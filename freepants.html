FREE PANTS

Find black pants out lying on the street or in a free box or donated. Cut out square pieces of cloth, also preferably scrap or felt bought in bulk, each square 3.75 inches on a side, 2 red, 2 orange, 2 yellow, 1 green, 1 blue, 1 purple. Cut out letters as shown. Cut out cardboard replicator with QR code back to this document. Stitch letters to pants. Make a second set of letters, put in pocket along with cardboard replicator. Use pants to advertise replication, replicate pants. Pants are not free until they self-replicate with trash as the feedstock. Find a public library makerspace with free sewing machine access or find a volunteer with a sewing machine who will let us replicate for free. Replicate and repeat. Add and evolve.
